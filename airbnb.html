About the Hosts

The residence is occupied by the Murphy family: Miriam, David and Paul. We are all very outgoing and friendly, we love meeting new people but also respect the privacy of all of our guests. We look forward to welcoming you to our home and hopefully giving you some great recommendations on things to see & do.

About the Area

We are located in a small village along the The Wild Atlantic Way, walking distance from pubs/restaurants, 10 minutes outside both Killarney & Killorglin towns, 5 minutes from the Gap Of Dunloe & Ireland's largest mountain Carrauntoohil & a short drive to spectacular beaches. If your looking for somewhere calm and cool to relax after a day of activities it's the perfect place for you.

About the Accommodation

The room is located in the left wing of the residence with access through the main door. It includes a super king bed, personal living room with fridge & private bathroom with shower & bath facilities.

About the Dog
